Wizard101

1 Month Membership: $9.95

Wizard101 is a massive multiplayer online role-playing game created by KingIsle entertainment. This MMORPG game is a family game. Players take on the role of students of witchcraft and wizardry to save the sprial. There are different schools of witchcraft such as Storm, Fire, Ice, Death, Balance, Life, and Myth. Players will battles enemies by casting spells. This game is mostly a card game that is free roaming.



Wizard101 is free to play! There is a Membership which unlocks tons of new areas and new quest. There is 2 currency in this game. Crowns and Gold. Crowns let users buy exclusive gear and packs. There are some good gold gear but most of the time Crown items are superior to gold items. Wizard101 is playable on Microsoft Windows, and Macintosh Operating Systems.
