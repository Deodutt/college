Bully

Bully: $14.99

Bully is an action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar Vancouver and was published by Rockstar Games. This game was released around 2006 and is still played today. This is a offline storymode that shows the life of a teen in high school. Throughout the story, players will control a character named, Jimmy Hopkins and will attend classes, do missions, and try to take over the school. This game gives users the option to free roam.



Bully is playable on Android, PS2, PS3, PS4, XBOX 360, Microsoft Windows, iOS, and Wii.
