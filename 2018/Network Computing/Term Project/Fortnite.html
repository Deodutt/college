Fortnite

1,000 V-Bucks: $10

Fortnite is a survival game that was created by Epic Games. It can be played on XBOX, PS4, PC, Mobile. There are 2 game modes, "Save the World" and "Battle Royale". Majority of people play "Battle Royale" mode because its Free to Play and fun.



Fortnite Battle Royale is free to play! In Battle Royale, players can buy a battle pass for a certain season which cost 950 V-Bucks. V-Bucks is a currency used in game. With V-Bucks you can buy skins, emotes, etc. The Battle pass gives players challenges and rewards them for getting obtaining tiers. Fortnite also has the game mode "Save the World" which is $40. Save the World Gamemode is a co-op sandbox survival game and is about exploration, scavenging items, crafting weapons, building fortified structures, and fighting waves of encroaching monsters.
