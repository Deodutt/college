Fortnite

Fortnite is a survival game that was created by Epic Games. It can be played on XBOX, PS4, PC, Mobile. There are 2 game modes, "Save the World" and "Battle Royale". Majority of people play "Battle Royale" mode because its Free to Play and fun.

Fortnite Battle Royale is free to play! In Battle Royale, players can buy a battle pass for a certain season which cost around $10. This battle pass gives players challenges and rewards them for getting different tiers in the battle pass. Fortnite also has the game mode "Save the World" which is $40.
