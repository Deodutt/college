Grand Theft Auto 5

Grand Theft Auto 5 is developed my Rockstar North Games. This is the 5th instalment to the Grand Theft Auto series. This is an action-adventure game that has 3 main characters that you basically play their lives as. You do missions such as robbing banks, stealing cars, and killing people. This game is intented for 18+ players

Grand Theft Auto 5 is around $30 dollars nowadays. It was originally $60 dollars from release. It is playable on XBOX, PS4, PC.
