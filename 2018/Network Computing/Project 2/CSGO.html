Counter Strike Global Offensive

CSGO is a multiplayer first person shooter game that is developed by Valve Corporation. Its a strong team based game. The objective is different based on the mode. Most of the time its either plant the bomb or kill all of the players in the other team. This game has a strong e-esports fan base. There are big tournaments that happen every year.

CSGO is $15 on steam. There are sales that happen every year which brings it down to around $7.
