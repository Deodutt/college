League of Legends

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena game. This game was created by Riot Games. This is a fast paced, competitive online game that has 2 teams fighting it out. The object of the game is to strategically destroy the enemey's nexus. A nexus is basically the heart of the team. Once you destroy that you win the game. League of legends also has a hierarchy ranking system. Bronze, silver, gold, platinum, diamond, master, challenger. Bronze is the lowest and challenger is the highest. There is a competitve e-sports scence that creates lots of attention. Some popular teams are Cloud9, Team SoloMid, Counter Logic Gaming, Team Liquid, etc. League is overall the number one free to play game.

League of Legends is free to play! League of Legends has a currency called "Riot Points" in the game which can be used to buy, champions, skins for champions, icons, ward skins, etc.
